Imposter — Age Suitability

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Imposter is a family-friendly party word game, suitable for all ages. We recommend it for everyone (rated 4+). It contains no objectionable content.

What the game is

Imposter is an in-person guessing-and-bluffing party game. Everyone gets the same secret word — except one player. Players say related words out loud, then talk and vote to find the imposter. Words come from 14 everyday themes (animals, food, sports, vehicles, nature, and more) drawn from a bank of 2,000+ carefully curated, child-appropriate words.

What the app does not contain

  • No violence, gore, or frightening content
  • No profanity, sexual, or mature themes
  • No gambling or simulated gambling
  • No advertisements and no third-party tracking
  • No in-app purchases
  • No open chat or messaging with strangers

Multiplayer & custom content

The optional "Play with Nearby Phones" mode connects devices in the same room over local Wi-Fi or Bluetooth only — there is no online matchmaking with strangers and no open chat. Players only ever see game words and the names typed in on each device.

Players may create their own custom word lists. These are stored privately on the device and are never shared online or sent to us.

Privacy

The app collects no personal data of any kind. See the Privacy Policy.
